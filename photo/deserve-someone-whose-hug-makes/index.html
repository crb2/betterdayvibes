You deserve someone whose hug makes you forget what was hurting your heart.

What this quote means

This quote is about relationships. It encourages you to choose connections that feel honest, peaceful, and mutual. It is a reminder to notice who brings calm to your life and who only asks you to shrink yourself. When you read "You deserve someone whose hug makes you forget what was hurting your heart.", let it become a small pause in your day: breathe, reflect, and choose the next step that feels healthier for your heart. A better life is often built through simple moments like this, where you return to yourself and decide to keep going with kindness.
