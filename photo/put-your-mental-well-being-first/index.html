Put Your Mental Well-Being First

Put Your Mental Well-Being First.

What this quote means

This quote is about relationships. It encourages you to choose connections that feel honest, peaceful, and mutual. It is a reminder to notice who brings calm to your life and who only asks you to shrink yourself. When you read "Put Your Mental Well-Being First.", let it become a small pause in your day: breathe, reflect, and choose the next step that feels healthier for your heart. A better life is often built through simple moments like this, where you return to yourself and decide to keep going with kindness.
