What this quote means

This quote is about motivation. It encourages you to keep moving with patience, courage, and a more hopeful mindset. It reminds you that even small shifts in thought and action can help create a better day. When you read "You're not stuck. You're being prepared for something greater than you can imagine. Trust the process.", let it become a small pause in your day: breathe, reflect, and choose the next step that feels healthier for your heart. A better life is often built through simple moments like this, where you return to yourself and decide to keep going with kindness.
