You're not your past you're the person deciding what happens next.

What this quote means

This quote is about healing. It encourages you to give yourself patience while you recover, grow, and release what has been heavy. It honors the fact that progress can be quiet, uneven, and still deeply meaningful. When you read "You're not your past you're the person deciding what happens next.", let it become a small pause in your day: breathe, reflect, and choose the next step that feels healthier for your heart. A better life is often built through simple moments like this, where you return to yourself and decide to keep going with kindness.
